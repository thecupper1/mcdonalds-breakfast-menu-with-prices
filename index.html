McDonald's Menu Breakfast Hours

The most important question! Generally, McDonald's serves breakfast from the time they open (usually 5:00 AM) until 10:30 AM on weekdays and 11:00 AM on weekends. However, hours can vary by location, so it's always best to check the McDonald's app for your local restaurant's specific times.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time does McDonald's start serving breakfast?

Most locations start serving breakfast as soon as they open, typically around 5:00 AM.

Can I get a burger during breakfast?

No, the main lunch/dinner menu is typically not available until after breakfast hours end.

Is McDonald's breakfast served all day?

Unfortunately, the all-day breakfast menu is no longer available in most locations.

What comes in a Big Breakfast® with Hotcakes?

It includes scrambled eggs, a sausage patty, a hash brown, a biscuit, and three hotcakes.
